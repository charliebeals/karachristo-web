The LEARN Partner Program

In order to provide the maximum level of service to the citizens of Lansing, we have partnered with several local physical therapists and athletic trainers, granting affordable access to our members.

Our Partners still have their own clients who are not members, and can grant a limited number of those clients concurrent access to all of our facilities. These clients can also purchase membership and day passes at a reduced price. We also have exclusive partnerships with one personal trainer and one physical therapist, and our members can utilize their services for free once a month and at a reduced cost beyond that.
