Stronger - Faster - Higher

We offer the most space and widest variety of activities of any facility in mid-michigan!

  • Running: A 1/2 mile indoor/covered track circling Meridian Mall provides running and walking abilities
  • Climbing: A 40-foot tall climbing wall allows 6 top-ropers to climb at once, while a 15-foot tall bouldering wall provides over 50 different routes
  • Lifting: Our state-of-the-art lifting room has every kind of machine you've ever heard of, and then a couple more. Our staff are all trained on the machines and can teach and advise you on the right machines to use to achive your personal goals.
  • Class space: We have 4 private studio spaces for you to host events in, such as yoga classes and fitness seminars. When not rented for private use, the rooms can be utilized by any members.
  • Partners: With your membership comes the priviledge of working with our physical therapist partners once a month, and access to the personal trainers (at their costs).
